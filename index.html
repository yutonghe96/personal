Intro

Full-time PhD student, part-time sci-fi writer, amateur photographer, and occasional globetrotter.

Physics

Gravitational waves (GWs) and cosmology are my main areas of interest. In particular, I study how GWs from the very early universe could be used to reveal the generating mechanisms at the time.

Sci-fi

Movies/TV or books alike, sci-fi is my favourite genre.

Some of my favourite authors are: Cixin Liu, Peter Watts, Ted Chiang, Arthur Clarke, and Ursula Le Guin.

In 2022, I started trying sci-fi writing myself and participated in the National Novel Writing Month (NaNoWriMo), an annual event that challenges aspiring writers to draft up a 50000-word novel in the month of November.

Well, I achieved about a quarter of that goal the first time around... 2023? We'll see!
